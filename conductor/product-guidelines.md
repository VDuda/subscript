# Product Guidelines

## 1. Prose Style
The documentation and user-facing text for SubScript will adopt a clear and concise prose style. The primary goal is direct communication, ensuring easy understanding for all users, especially given the technical nature of Bitcoin. All content will emphasize the user experience (UX) and clearly articulate how SubScript solves specific user problems, making complex concepts accessible.

## 2. Visual Identity
The visual identity of SubScript will be bold and futuristic. This will be achieved through the use of dark themes, vibrant accents, and strong typography. The aim is to create a cutting-edge, crypto-native aesthetic that reflects the innovative nature of the project.

## 3. Brand Messaging
SubScript's brand messaging will primarily focus on simplicity and ease of use. The communication will stress how SubScript makes recurring payments on Bitcoin straightforward, accessible, and hassle-free, highlighting its user-friendly approach to a novel financial mechanism.
